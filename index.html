// 002 — HOW IT WORKS

How it
Works.

Create

Pick a theme (Sci-Fi, Anime, or Tribute) and track hours via Hackatime.

Ship It

Submit your project for review by Master Alchistants to earn theme currency.

Mix Potions

Convert currencies into Potion Mix. Mixing multiple types grants bonuses.

Dynamic Shop

Spend your Potion Mix on rewards. New items are added weekly based on your suggestions!

// 003 — THEMES

The Three Themes
of Season 1.

01

Sci-Fi

Create a project based on Sci-Fi, it can be anything — a website, an app, a game, a robot, anything. As long as it's based on Sci-Fi, it counts.

02

Anime

Make something related to your favourite Anime character, it can be a website, an app, a game, a robot, anything. As long as it's based on Anime, it counts.

03

Tribute

Make a project that pays tribute to something — it can be a person, a group, an event, anything. As long as it's a tribute, it counts.

// 004 — FAQ

Frequently
Asked.

What is a 'ship'?

01

A 'ship' is a completed project you submit for review. It must be functional, publicly accessible, and built during the season. Shipped projects are reviewed by Master Alchistants who award you theme currency based on quality and effort.

How do I participate?

02

Join the Alchemize Slack, pick one of the three season themes, start tracking your hours via Hackatime, and ship your project before the deadline. That's it — no sign-up fee, no gatekeeping.

Who is Eligible?

03

Alchemize is open to all high school students globally. You don't need any prior experience — just curiosity and the drive to build something cool.

How is time tracked?

04

Time is tracked automatically through Hackatime, a free coding time tracker. Install it in your editor and it logs your hours in the background — no manual entry needed.

What is Hack Club?

05

Hack Club is a global nonprofit network of high school coding clubs and makers. Alchemize is a Hack Club program, meaning it's built for teen hackers by teen hackers.

Where can I find more information?

06

Head over to the Alchemize Slack or check out the official Hack Club page. You can also reach out directly via the contact section below — we're happy to help.
